Structural BIM Service

Specializing in structural BIM services, we expedite fabrication and construction through Revit, ArchiCAD, and AutoCAD modeling. Our expertise includes delivering precise shop drawings for steel and RCC, ensuring effective collaboration and seamless iterative design modifications.

Architecture Work Stages.

We work according to principles and are always careful in every step before implementing an architectural project.

LOD 100 - Conceptual Design

At LOD 100, we provide high-level conceptual models that outline the project's overall massing, shape, and scale. Ideal for early-stage planning, our models help stakeholders visualize the design intent and make informed decisions from the start. Trust us to lay a strong foundation for your project's success.

LOD 200 - Schematic Design

Our LOD 200 models include generalized systems, assemblies, and approximate quantities. Ideal for design development, coordination, and early cost estimation, these models begin to take shape with meaningful geometry and spatial relationships.

LOD 300 - Design Development

At this level, we provide accurate modeling with defined geometry, location, and dimensions. Suitable for construction documentation and coordination, LOD 300 ensures your project is fully detailed and ready for real-world application.

LOD 350 - Construction Documentation

LOD 350 includes all elements modeled with detailed connections and interfaces between systems. Perfect for clash detection, coordination, and installation planning—this is where design meets constructability.

LOD 400 - Fabrication & Assembly

We deliver fabrication-ready models with precise details for manufacturing, assembly, and installation. LOD 400 is ideal for contractors and fabricators who need accurate, data-rich models to build from directly.

LOD 500 – As-Built Models/ Operational
