At The Zenith Visions, we provide cost-effective and tailored pricing for a comprehensive range of architectural and engineering services. Our core expertise lies in Scan to BIM, where we accurately convert point cloud data into intelligent 3D BIM models using Revit or AutoCAD. This includes detailed architectural, structural, and MEP modeling, ensuring precise coordination and documentation for as-built and renovation projects. Our team is proficient in handling complex geometries and delivering models at various Levels of Detail (LOD100 to LOD500), customized to your project needs.

In addition to Scan to BIM, we specialize in Scan to CAD, CAD to BIM, PDF or hand sketch to CAD/BIM, and full-scope 2D drafting and 3D modeling services. We also deliver Revit family creation, topography modeling, facade detailing, kitchen cabinetry development with material callouts, interior CD and DD sets, millwork and joinery detailing, and high-quality rendering, animation, and walkthrough visualizations. All services are executed by our experienced team of architects, civil engineers, and BIM specialists using industry-standard tools such as Revit, AutoCAD, ArchiCAD, SketchUp, Chief Architect, Enscape, Lumion, and Twinmotion.

Our pricing is project-specific and depends on factors such as project size, scope, modeling complexity, building type, number of floors, LOD/LOA requirements, coordination needs (architectural, structural, MEPFS), sheet sizes, manpower, and turnaround time. Once we understand your requirements, we provide a customized proposal detailing the scope of work, pricing, timeline, deliverables, progress milestones, and communication workflow. We have an option in the below URL where customers are able to share the link of images, pdf, or point cloud data for a better understanding, which could help us to quickly generate an exact quotation and delivery timeline.

To receive a personalized quote, please send your project files and scope of work to INFO@THEZENITHVISIONS.COM or call us at +91 87700 93683. We are committed to delivering high-quality, reliable, and affordable BIM and CAD solutions tailored to your project goals.

We offer three flexible engagement models: Fixed Price for clearly defined scopes, Hourly Rate for dynamic and ongoing needs, and Dedicated Resources for long-term or large-scale engagements. This ensures you get the right level of support while maintaining budget control and delivery efficiency.
